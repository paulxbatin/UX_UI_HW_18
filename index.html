Portfolio ✹ Work

Carevan: app for allocating travel costs

UX/UI Project

Carevan is a travel app specializing in the allocation of total trip costs. The project is an iteration on an idea that would benefit the abundance of travelers. The concept of splitting costs while traveling is often attainable among the usage of multiple apps leading the design to consolidate the experience into a one stop shop.

FLEXP: reward based fitness tracking app

UX/UI Project

FLEXP is a fitness app that gives you an opportunity to track your workouts to earn in-app and real life rewards. The app urges users to participate in activities in order to earn ‘EXP’ points. These can be used to upgrade the avatar in app, or earn real rewards like gift cards. You can invite friends or compete with others according to the leaderboard. This collaboration was based on users’ needs for accountability and inspiration.

BWF: website redesign for non-profit

UX/UI Redesign

The Bunny World Foundation serves as a pillar against animal cruelty. They offer adoption services and a database of information regarding bunny care and maintenance. The redesign prioritizes human-centered design and the importance of hierarchy and usability. We conducted heuristic evaluations to determine user insights to elaborate a user-friendly flow, focusing on features we prioritized based on the probability of usage.
